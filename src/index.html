Process

In the fall of 2022 I build a new website for Public Leadership Foundation. I designed this website in Figma used 11ty in combination with Netlify CMS to generate the webpages. The advantage of this is a fast and secure website. In addition, no external server is required to host the CMS. The customer manages the content that matters to her/him, such as blogs, events and workshops, and I'll take care of the rest. During development I used HTML, SCSS, Javascript, Nunjuck's templates and JSON. We will go live as soon as the content is ready. If you are curious how it all functions.
